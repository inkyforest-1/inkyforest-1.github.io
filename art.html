My Artwork

I have a basic pixel art style. 3D art is not my strong suit.

The Camel: A picture of a camel pixelated camel I made using Piskel. The camel was supposed to have at least one ear but it was hard, I didn't include them.


The Bottle of Lotion: A bottle of lotion I made using Piskel.


The Loon: An image of a loon I made using Piskel.I made this to test my skills and see if I could make objects/animals that are odd shaped, since I'm mainly good at even shapes such as circles and squares.


Donut & Mug: A 3D donut and mug I tried to make in Blender for a class assignment. The assignment was to follow the Blender Donut Tutorial were you create a 3D donut with a 3D mug. I had a hard time with it. I didn't even bother trying to add color to it.
