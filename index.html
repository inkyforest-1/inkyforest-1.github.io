About Me

I am a senior in a gaming and multi media class. I have passion for writing exspecialy linear stories. I worked in a group of 6(including me) on a game called "Echo Chambers". I worked with another person on the game lore. The person I worked with on the game lore was in charge of the game. They came up with the idea for the game and some of the story. The leader would give me pieces of lore that he wanted in the actual game and he would have me build the story based on what he gave me.
