About Me

I am a senior in a gaming and multi media class where I worked in a group of 5 on a game called the Echo Chambers. I was one of the main writers but I wasn't the one who started the idea. I built off of his words with my own words and he ended up liking my words more.

What I want to do

I want to write stories as a hobby not as a job. Stories with a happy ending, stories that refer to something else that many people just don't care about or can't stand to listen to.
